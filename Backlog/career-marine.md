# Add Marine career

Add `data/careers/marine.yaml` so Marine is selectable in career selection.

## Scope

- Qualification: END 6+. DM−2 if aged 30 or more (aging not yet implemented — see `Backlog/aging.md`).
- Assignments (each with its own Survival / Advancement):
  - Support — Survival END 5+, Advancement EDU 7+
  - Star Marine — Survival END 6+, Advancement EDU 6+
  - Ground Assault — Survival END 7+, Advancement EDU 5+
- Fill in service skills, per-assignment skill tables, events, mishaps, and rank table per the Marine career entry in the core rules.
- Military career — depends on commission/officer backlog items for full fidelity.
